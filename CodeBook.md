#Codebook

##Description

Data is from 30 volunteers, each performing the same 6 activities. Age range of volunteers is 19-48 yrs. Each volunteer is wearing a smartphone (Samsung Galaxy S II) on the waist. 

From the smartphone's embedded accelerometer and gyroscope, we have data on 3-axial linear acceleration and 3-axial angular velocity at a constant rate of 50Hz. 

[1][2]

##Grouping/IDs:
- Activity - activity performed by subject
- SubjectID - subject ID

##Measurements:
-Normalized data, i.e. within [-1,1]

Notations for the variables are:
- t: time

- f: frequency

- Acc: accelerometer

- Gyro: gyroscope

- Jerk: jerk signals

- Gravity: gravity

- Mag: magnitude 

- X, Y, Z denotes the 3-axial signals

- Mean - mean

- Std - standard deviation
